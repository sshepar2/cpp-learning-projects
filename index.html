Projects

01

link-simulator

A BPSK/QPSK digital communications link simulation. Generates random bit sequences, modulates to IQ symbols, transmits through an AWGN channel, and computes BER across a sweep of SNR values. Outputs a BER vs SNR curve for link budget analysis.

Scope and standards: These projects are written in the context of simulation and ground support software, where standard C++ features including exceptions, STL containers, and dynamic memory allocation are appropriate. They do not follow safety-critical coding standards such as JSF++ or MISRA C++, which apply to flight-certified and embedded systems and impose additional constraints including no exceptions, no dynamic allocation, and bounded loops.
